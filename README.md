The AI Receptionist app is an app that saves customers the hassle of endlessly trying to connect with receptionist on the phone while they are staying at the hotel. You need towels, pillows, blankets, shampoo, help with anything, just ask AI receptionist and help is right on the way.

This demo app contains only these Items are services listed, more can be added later.

Items
Towels
Blankets
Pillow Cases
Pillow
Toilet Paper
Face Towels
Bath Robes
Shower Caps
Shampoo
Water Bottles
Iron
Remote Control
ToothBrush
Lotion

Services
Help with Thermostat
Help to open door
Help to carry luggage
Help to control noise
Help to lift customer up
